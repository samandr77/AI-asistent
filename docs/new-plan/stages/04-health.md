# Stage 04 - Health And Energy

## Goal

Build a health and energy module that tracks core signals, explains trends, and feeds energy-aware planning.

## Features

- Health dashboard with score, 7/30/90-day trends, activity rings, and quick navigation.
- Sleep tracking with bedtime, wake time, phases, duration, and factors.
- Activity tracking: steps, distance, active minutes, calories, stand reminders.
- Workout diary: strength/cardio/yoga/stretching, sets, PRs, plans, muscle groups.
- Readiness score from HRV, resting heart rate, sleep, and load.
- Nutrition diary with calories, macros, micronutrients, water, photo/barcode placeholder.
- Biomarkers: HRV, heart rate, SpO2, breathing, pressure, sugar, cholesterol.
- Stress and mood tracking with meditation and breathing exercise records.
- Weight, body composition, metabolism, and goal projection.
- Medical records: labs, medications, visits, vaccines, doctor summary.
- Optional cycle and reproductive health module.
- AI health insights with baseline comparison and safety framing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Health module works with manual entry before external device integrations.
- Insights cite source metrics and avoid diagnosis language.
- Optional reproductive health module can be disabled completely.
