# Stage 05 - Finance

## Goal

Create a personal finance module that works through dashboards, manual input, imports, documents, and conversational AI.

## Features

- Dashboard with balances, monthly spend, remaining budget, net worth, transactions, and alerts.
- Transactions with categorization, manual edit, notes, filters, search, and income/expense split.
- Budget planning by category with daily/monthly views, alerts, rollover, redistribution, templates.
- Financial goals and savings with target amount, date, account link, contribution advice.
- Subscriptions and recurring payments with detection, next charge, reminders, and monthly total.
- Analytics: category charts, period comparison, cash flow, trends, filters.
- Debts and credits with balance, rate, payment, next date, payoff calculator, schedule.
- Investments/assets with value history, allocation, dividends, ROI, growth.
- Net worth history and projection.
- Income tracking by source and monthly comparison.
- Taxes and financial documents with deadlines, deductions, receipts, and calendar.
- AI finance chat for entry, anomalies, savings advice, receipt interpretation, and budget load warnings.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Manual finance entry works before bank integrations.
- AI can ask clarifying questions when category or purpose is ambiguous.
- Tax/investment outputs include safety framing and do not pretend to be professional advice.
