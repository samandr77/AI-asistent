## Description (≤4000 chars)

Second Brain is a personal AI assistant that turns voice notes and scattered thoughts into structured tasks and goals.

What it does
- Voice dump: record what's in your head — AI parses it into tasks with priorities and life spheres (work, family, health, study, travel, finance).
- First-class goals: set weekly/monthly/yearly goals, link tasks to them, track progress over time.
- Evening reflection: 60 seconds a day — mood, energy, alignment to goals. Streaks included.
- Today's Top-3: AI picks 3 tasks for the day based on your goals and peak productivity hours.

Privacy-first
- Your data is yours. Row-level security enforces that only you can read your tasks.
- No ads. No cross-app tracking.
- Premium subscription is managed by App Store; cancel anytime from Settings.

Premium unlocks
- Unlimited voice dumps (free tier: 10/day)
- Unlimited goals (free: 3 active)
- Full task history (free: last 30 days)
- Access to advanced AI models for more accurate dump parsing

Account deletion
Delete your account from Profile → "Delete account". All data is permanently erased within 30 days. No recovery.
