## Full description (≤4000 chars)

Second Brain is an AI assistant that turns voice notes into structured tasks, goals, and daily plans.

What's inside
- **Voice dump** — speak what's on your mind; AI parses it into tasks with priorities and spheres (work, family, health, study, travel, finance).
- **Goals** — set them, link tasks, track progress over time.
- **Evening reflection** — 60 seconds a day: mood, energy, alignment to goals. Streaks included.
- **Today's Top-3** — AI picks 3 tasks for today based on your goals.

Privacy
- Row-level security in DB: only you can see your data.
- No ads. No cross-app tracking.
- Premium subscription via Google Play, cancel anytime.

Premium unlocks
- Unlimited voice dumps (free: 10/day)
- Unlimited goals (free: 3 active)
- Full task history (free: last 30 days)
- Access to advanced AI models

Delete account from Profile. All data erased within 30 days.

Support: support@second-brain.app
